# Juice Shots & Drinks

**Green Tea Fat-Burning Shot**  
Green tea extract, lemon/lime, cinnamon, honey, hot water  
Directions: Add 1 tsp green tea extract + juice of ½ lemon/lime + 1 tsp honey + pinch cinnamon to 8-12oz hot water. Stir and drink. (2-3 cups daily, morning preferred; limit extras.)[cite: 2]

**Ginger Cayenne Cinnamon Drink**  
Ginger, cayenne pepper, cinnamon, water  
Directions: Blend 8-12oz diced ginger with ½ cup water to paste. Boil 1 gallon water, add paste + 1 tsp cayenne + 2 tbsp cinnamon. Steep 2+ hours, strain, dilute to 1 gallon if needed. Refrigerate up to 7 days. (8-12oz 1-2x/day, 5 days/week.)[cite: 2]

**Raw Apple Cider Vinegar Drink**  
Unfiltered ACV with mother, warm water, optional honey  
Directions: Mix 1-2 tsp ACV + optional 1 tsp honey in 8-12oz warm water. Drink daily.[cite: 2]

# Breakfast / Eggs

**Menemen**  
Tomatoes, onions, eggs (Turkish-style scrambled)  
Directions: Sauté onions and tomatoes until saucy, add beaten eggs, scramble gently until set. Season to taste.[cite: 2]

**Best Egg Bake**  
Eggs, cottage cheese, high-protein breakfast bake (meal prep friendly)  
Directions: Mix eggs + cottage cheese + desired add-ins (veggies/meat/cheese). Bake in dish at 350°F until set (about 30-40 min). Portion for the week.[cite: 2]

**Protein Pancake Bowl**  
Egg, yogurt, milk, flour, protein powder, baking powder, honey, fruit topping  
Directions: Mix 1 egg + 50g yogurt + 70ml milk + 35g flour + 25g protein powder + ½ tsp baking powder + 1 tsp honey. Pour into oven-safe bowl, top with fruit, bake at 350°F for 20-25 min. Drizzle extra honey if desired.[cite: 2]

**Carrot Cake Baked Oats**  
Grated carrot, mashed bananas, oats/oat flour, vegan vanilla protein, spices, milk, pecans, raisins; topped with high-protein yogurt  
Directions: Mix all (except topping) in greased dish. Bake at 350°F for 35-40 min. Cool, top with vanilla protein yogurt + pecans. Serves 6; fridge up to 4 days.[cite: 2]

**Healthy Carrot Oat Cake**  
Mashed banana, oats, vanilla caramel protein powder, grated carrot, cinnamon, baking powder, walnuts, almond milk; topped with Greek yogurt + walnuts  
Directions: Mix all, pour into parchment-lined loaf tin. Bake at 350°F for 30 min. Cool, top with yogurt + walnuts. Serves 4.[cite: 2]

**Blueberry Oatmeal Cake**  
Oats (or oat flour), Greek yogurt/eggs for protein, blueberries, mashed banana or maple, baking powder, cinnamon  
Directions: Mix oats + yogurt/eggs + mashed banana/maple + baking powder + cinnamon + blueberries. Bake in dish at 350°F for 30-40 min until set. Slice; high-protein breakfast/snack (moist, whole-food based).[cite: 2]

# Salads

**Classic French Green Salad with Jacques Pépin's Vinaigrette**  
Butter lettuce, mixed tender greens, radishes, cucumber ribbons, fresh herbs (chives/parsley/tarragon)  
Vinaigrette: chopped garlic, Dijon mustard, salt, pepper, wine vinegar, extra-virgin olive oil  
Directions: Tear lettuce/add greens/veggies/herbs. Shake vinaigrette (2 tsp garlic + 2 tbsp Dijon + ½ tsp salt + ¼ tsp pepper + ¼ cup vinegar + 1 cup oil) in jar. Drizzle lightly, toss gently, serve immediately.[cite: 2]

**Caesar Salad (Jacques Pépin & Julia Child Style)**  
Romaine hearts, garlic croutons, Parmesan, classic dressing (egg yolk, garlic, Dijon mustard, lemon, optional anchovy)  
Directions: Mash garlic, mix dressing with yolk/mustard/lemon/oil. Toss romaine with dressing, top with croutons/cheese. Serve whole leaves or torn.[cite: 2]

**High-Protein Egg Salad**  
Hard-boiled eggs, avocado, red onion, chives, low-fat cottage cheese, lemon juice, salt/pepper  
Directions: Mash all together in bowl. Adjust seasoning. Serve on crackers, toast, or lettuce leaves. (~26g protein.)[cite: 2]

**Avocado Egg Salad Bowl**  
Half avocado, 2 boiled eggs, diced onion, cottage cheese, chili crisp oil (or olive oil)  
Directions: Mash/chop and mix all. Serve fresh or refrigerate up to 4 days. (~27g protein, 491 cal.)[cite: 2]

**Creamy Chopped Chicken Salad Stuffed Pitas**  
Chopped chicken, creamy dressing (likely mayo/Greek yogurt base), veggies, stuffed in pitas  
Directions: Mix chopped chicken + creamy elements + add-ins. Stuff into pitas. (Full details via source link if needed.)[cite: 2]

**No-Bore Tuna Cucumber Rolls**  
Tuna salad, cucumber strips (sliced thin like sushi)  
Directions: Peel cucumber into strips, overlap slightly, add tuna salad across, roll like sushi, slice into pieces. Ready in <10 min.[cite: 2]

# Main Dishes / Sides

**Vegan Thai Peanut Stir-Fry**  
Tofu, mixed stir-fry vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas), garlic, ginger, tamari sauce, lime juice, peanut butter, coconut milk, red pepper flakes, chopped peanuts[cite: 1]  
Directions: Sauté garlic and ginger in a pan. Add tofu and stir-fry until golden. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender. Stir in tamari sauce, lime juice, peanut butter, coconut milk, and red pepper flakes. Serve over cooked rice or quinoa.[cite: 1]

**Vegan Chickpea Curry**  
Cooked chickpeas, diced tomatoes, coconut milk, diced onion, minced garlic, grated ginger, curry powder, turmeric, cumin, salt, and pepper[cite: 1]  
Directions: Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in a pot until fragrant. Add diced tomatoes, coconut milk, cooked chickpeas, and spices. Simmer until flavors meld together.[cite: 1]

**Vegan Lentil Tacos**  
Cooked lentils seasoned with taco seasoning, corn tortillas, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, salsa, lime wedges[cite: 1]  
Directions: Fill corn tortillas with seasoned lentils and top with lettuce, tomatoes, avocado, and salsa. Serve with lime wedges on the side.[cite: 1]

**Sautéed Eggplant Rolls (Jacques Pépin)**  
Eggplant slices, mushrooms, onion, garlic, golden raisins, Monterey Jack cheese, olive oil, fresh tomatoes for topping  
Directions: Sauté thin eggplant rounds in oil until pliable. Sauté onion/garlic/mushrooms, mix with chopped cheese + raisins. Fill eggplant slices, roll up, arrange, top with chopped tomatoes. Serve warm as main or side.[cite: 2]

**Refried Pinto Beans**  
Pinto beans, onion, garlic, salt, garlic salt, oil  
Directions: Boiling washed beans with onion/garlic in water until soft, add salts, simmer low. In pan, heat oil with onion, add beans + broth, mash to consistency, add broth if dry.[cite: 2]

**Late Night Kebab (Shawarma-Style Wrap)**  
Extra lean beef mince, shawarma seasoning, mini pitta, onion, tomatoes, lettuce, hot sauce, spray oil  
Directions: Spread mince on pitta, season, spray oil. Fry meat-side down 4-5 min, flip. Wrap with veggies/hot sauce in foil like burrito. (~450 cal, high protein.)[cite: 2]

**One-Pan Mediterranean Chicken**  
Chicken thighs, Greek yogurt marinade (garam masala, garlic, lemon), bell peppers, onion, cherry tomatoes, dill sauce  
Directions: Cube + marinate chicken. Add veggies to pan, top with chicken, bake at 375°F 15-20 min + broil 5-7 min. Serve with yogurt-dill sauce on pita. (4 servings, ~46g protein each.)[cite: 2]

**Oven-Roasted Marinated Chicken**  
Boneless chicken thighs, processed onion, lemon juice, tomato paste, Greek yogurt, spices (thyme, garlic powder, urfa/Aleppo pepper)  
Directions: Mix marinade, coat chicken (marinate 2-6+ hours). Pack tightly in loaf pan, bake at 425°F for 50 min. Drain juices, slice thin. Fridge 4-5 days.[cite: 2]

**High-Protein Crispy Korean Popcorn Chicken**  
Chicken breast, soy sauce, egg, cornstarch; glaze (soy, gochujang, honey, etc.); sticky rice  
Directions: Coat chicken pieces, airfry/oven at high temp until crispy. Toss in glaze. Serve with seasoned sticky rice + garnishes. (4 servings, ~50g protein each.)[cite: 2]

**Easy 3-Ingredient Chicken Nuggies**  
Ground chicken, eggs, ground sourdough crackers (or breadcrumbs), salt/pepper, EVOO  
Directions: Mix ground chicken + eggs + seasoning. Form, coat in crumbs, bake at 400°F ~20 min (flip halfway). Serve with sauces.[cite: 2]

**High Protein Honey BBQ Chicken Mac & Cheese**  
Chicken breast (seasoned), macaroni, fat-free evaporated milk, light cream cheese, light cheddar, honey + sugar-free BBQ  
Directions: Season/cook chicken. Boil pasta, mix with milk/cheese/BBQ sauce. Combine with chicken. (4 servings, ~59g protein each.)[cite: 2]

**Chicken Shawarma Sheet Pan Dinner**  
Chicken thighs, chickpeas/veggies (onion, bell peppers, carrots), Greek yogurt marinade, shawarma spices (cumin, paprika, coriander, garlic)  
Directions: Marinate chicken in yogurt + spices. Toss with chickpeas/veggies on sheet pan, roast at 425°F for 30-35 min until crispy. Serve as is, over rice/salad, or in wraps. (High protein ~40g/serving, high fiber, easy one-pan.)[cite: 2]

# Desserts / Snacks

**Giant Protein Reese’s**  
Greek yogurt, vanilla protein powder, peanut butter powder, Lily’s chocolate chips  
Directions: Mix yogurt + protein + PB powder. Melt chips, spread on top, freeze 20 min. Crack and eat. (~51g protein, 342 cal.)[cite: 2]

**Homemade Reese’s PB Pumpkins**  
Natural peanut butter, almond flour, maple syrup, chocolate chips, coconut oil  
Directions: Mix PB + flour + syrup to dough, freeze briefly, cut pumpkin shapes. Melt chocolate + oil, dip, freeze to set. Store fridge/freezer.[cite: 2]

**$0.94 Double Chocolate Protein Bars**  
Oats, PB powder, whey protein, cocoa, Greek yogurt, almond milk; chocolate + coconut oil topping  
Directions: Mix base, flatten in container. Melt chocolate + oil, spread on top. Fridge 30 min, cut into 5. (~19g protein each.)[cite: 2]

**Blueberry Muffins**  
Bananas, eggs, crunchy peanut butter, blueberries, baking powder  
Directions: Mash bananas, mix with eggs + PB + baking powder + blueberries. Bake in muffin tins (temp/time standard ~350°F 20-25 min).[cite: 2]

**Healthy Carrot Cake**  
Overripe banana, Greek yogurt, maple syrup, eggs, vanilla, spices, grated carrots, oats/oat flour/coconut flour; cream cheese frosting  
Directions: Mix all, bake at 350°F for 40 min. Frost with cream cheese + lemon + maple. Moist and whole-food based.[cite: 2]

**Protein PB Chocolate Balls**  
PBfit/PB2, unsweetened applesauce, Lily’s dark chocolate chips, coconut oil  
Directions: Mix PB powder + applesauce, form 11 balls, fridge 15-30 min. Melt chocolate + oil, roll balls in it, fridge again 10-15 min. (~5.5g protein each.)[cite: 2]

**Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Cups**  
Greek yogurt, French vanilla whey, peanut butter powder + water, chocolate chips + coconut oil  
Directions: Mix yogurt + protein + PB mixture. Melt chocolate + oil, layer or dip. Freeze/chill to set. (6 cups, high protein.)[cite: 2]

**Banana Bread Protein Bites**  
Mashed banana, rolled oats, peanut/almond butter, vanilla protein powder, cinnamon, optional chocolate chips/nuts  
Directions: Mash banana, mix with oats + nut butter + protein powder + cinnamon. Roll into balls, fridge 15-30 min to firm. No-bake, high-protein snack (~energy boost like banana bread).[cite: 2]

# Sauces / Dips

**Hot Honey Ranch**  
Avocado oil, chili flakes, sesame seeds, garlic, Greek yogurt, honey, dill, ranch seasoning (garlic/onion powder, parsley, mustard powder)  
Directions: Heat oil, pour over chili/sesame/garlic/salt to make chili oil, cool. Mix with yogurt + honey + dill + seasoning. Refrigerate up to 10 days. (Hot; reduce flakes for milder.)[cite: 2]

**Homemade Buttermilk Ranch**  
Fat-free Greek yogurt, low-fat sour cream, buttermilk, lemon juice, seasonings (chives, parsley, dill, garlic/onion powder)  
Directions: Blend all until smooth. Tweak with ranch powder or whey for extra protein. (~29g protein per bowl, 215 cal.)[cite: 2]

**Top 3 Healthy Sauces for Chicken**  
1. Creamy Avocado Lime: avocado, Greek yogurt, lime, garlic, salt/pepper.  
2. Zesty Cilantro Yogurt: Greek yogurt, cilantro, lime, cumin, garlic.  
3. Smoky Chipotle Lime: light mayo/Greek yogurt, chipotle in adobo, lime, honey.  
Directions (each): Blend/mix ingredients, adjust consistency with water. Drizzle on chicken.[cite: 2]

**Protein Yum Yum Sauce**
Fat-free Greek yogurt, light mayo, ketchup, rice vinegar, honey, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, fat-free milk (as needed)
Directions: Add all ingredients + 2 tbsp milk to a jar. Stir until smooth. Adjust with more milk 1-2 tbsp at a time for desired consistency. Refrigerate 15–30 min for flavors to meld. (~29g protein full jar, 428 cal)[cite: 2]
